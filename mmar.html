National Recognition

CyberGirls has been honored with numerous awards and recognitions for our commitment to empowering women in cybersecurity. Our partnerships with leading institutions like Limkokwing University, IPAM, and FBC have strengthened our ability to provide world-class education and training opportunities.

Through these collaborations, we've successfully trained over 500 women, with many graduates now working in prestigious cybersecurity positions across the country. Our initiative continues to set new standards in technology education and women's empowerment.
